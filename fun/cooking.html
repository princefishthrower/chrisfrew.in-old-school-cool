Galaxy Ice Cream

  • Icecream:
  • 14 oz. can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 pint heavy whipping cream
  •  
  • Mixed in:
  • 1 bar Hershey's chocolate, crushed
  • 1 Zero bar, sliced thin
  • 1/2 bag of Flipz chocolate covered pretzles
  • 1/4 bag of white chocolate chips
  • 1 bag of mini Reese's
  • 1 bag of M&M's
  •  
  • Directions:
  • Wip cream, mix in condensed milk, mix in toppings by hand.
  • Freeze for at least 24 to acheive best consistency
