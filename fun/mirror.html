Work & Projects

The following are links to a few of my projects:


Numerical
Tracers

My first M.S. project. We treat molecules as graphs (atoms as nodes and bonds as edges) and apply a hierarchical pattern matching algorithm to determine the symmetries of structures of the molecules in each reaction, ultimately determining the mass amounts of each location in each molecule at every timestep of combustion. (***Note: the repo itself is private; the link brings up a screenshot of the repo as a show and tell.)
